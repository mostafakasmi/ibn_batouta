our history

Ibn Battuta, a high school located in the city of Aklim, began operating officially as a high school in 1993, but it existed before in the form of a preparatory school in 1982. And it can be summed up like this:

1982-1993 : Preparatory (11 years)
1993-2023 : High School (30 years old)
The number of current students: 750 students
number of current professors: 45 professors
